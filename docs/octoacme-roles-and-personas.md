# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## QA Engineers

### Role Summary
QA Engineers ensure software quality through comprehensive testing, verification of acceptance criteria, and defect prevention. They collaborate with developers and product teams to deliver reliable products.

### Responsibilities
- Design and execute test plans and test cases
- Verify features meet acceptance criteria and quality standards
- Identify, document, and track defects
- Participate in design and code reviews from a quality perspective
- Maintain test automation and CI/CD quality gates

### Goals
- Ensure high product quality and reliability
- Catch defects early in the development cycle
- Maintain comprehensive test coverage

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- Defect reports and testing status updates
- Collaborate with Developers on bug fixes and test coverage
- Work with Product Managers to clarify acceptance criteria

---

## UX/UI Designer

### Role Summary
UX/UI Designers create user experiences and interfaces that meet usability, accessibility, and business requirements. They ensure products are intuitive, aesthetically pleasing, and aligned with user needs.

### Responsibilities
- Collaborate with Product Managers and Developers to define user flows and interface behavior
- Create wireframes, prototypes, and high-fidelity designs
- Conduct user research and usability testing
- Ensure the product meets accessibility standards
- Maintain design systems and style guides

### Goals
- Deliver intuitive and accessible user experiences
- Reduce user friction and support adoption
- Maintain design consistency across the product

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews with Product Managers and Developers
- User research findings and usability test results
- Collaborate with Developers on technical feasibility
- Coordinate with QA Engineers for acceptance of visual and interaction details

---

## DevOps Engineer

### Role Summary
DevOps Engineers ensure reliable, automated, and secure delivery of code to production. They manage infrastructure, deployment pipelines, and production monitoring.

### Responsibilities
- Manage CI/CD pipelines, infrastructure-as-code, and deployment automation
- Monitor production systems and coordinate incident response
- Work closely with Developers for build and deployment needs
- Ensure security and compliance practices in the delivery process
- Maintain system reliability, scalability, and performance

### Goals
- Achieve fast, reliable, and safe deployments
- Minimize production incidents and downtime
- Automate operational tasks and infrastructure management

### Typical Communication
- Collaborate with Developers to resolve build and deployment issues
- Notify Project Managers of infrastructure risks and capacity needs
- Partner with Security Lead on security implementations
- Coordinate with QA Engineers on test environment management

---

## Data Analyst

### Role Summary
Data Analysts transform raw data into actionable insights that guide team decisions. They track metrics, analyze trends, and support data-driven decision making.

### Responsibilities
- Define and track success metrics with Product Managers and Project Managers
- Build dashboards and run analysis for features or releases
- Identify trends, anomalies, and opportunities in product usage
- Support analytics instrumentation and data quality
- Provide insights to inform product roadmap and prioritization

### Goals
- Enable data-driven decision making across the team
- Ensure visibility into product performance and user behavior
- Identify opportunities for optimization and growth

### Typical Communication
- Deliver periodic reports to Product Managers and Project Managers
- Support Developers with analytics instrumentation requirements
- Share insights during sprint planning and retrospectives
- Collaborate with Support/Customer Success on user feedback analysis

---

## Support/Customer Success

### Role Summary
Support/Customer Success professionals act as user advocates, ensuring customer feedback and problems are channeled back to the team and addressed effectively.

### Responsibilities
- Collect, organize, and share user feedback and feature requests
- Partner with Product Managers for prioritization of customer issues
- Assist in drafting user communication and documentation updates
- Track customer satisfaction and escalate critical issues
- Support customer onboarding and adoption

### Goals
- Ensure customer needs are heard and addressed
- Maximize customer satisfaction and retention
- Provide timely resolution to customer issues

### Typical Communication
- Provide customer insights to Product Managers for roadmap planning
- Alert Project Managers and QA Engineers to incidents and trends
- Coordinate with Developers on reproducing and resolving customer issues
- Share feedback with UX/UI Designers on usability challenges

---

## Security Lead

### Role Summary
Security Leads oversee the security of systems and processes, reviewing risks, coordinating security incidents, and ensuring best practices are followed.

### Responsibilities
- Run threat modeling and proactive security reviews
- Respond to and coordinate security incidents
- Ensure secure coding practices, compliance, and security standards
- Review architecture and code changes for security implications
- Maintain security documentation and training materials

### Goals
- Minimize security risks and vulnerabilities
- Ensure compliance with security standards and regulations
- Foster a security-aware culture across the team

### Typical Communication
- Collaborate with Developers and DevOps Engineers on implementation and incident response
- Report security risks to Product Managers and Project Managers for prioritization
- Conduct security reviews during design and code review phases
- Provide security guidance and training to the team
